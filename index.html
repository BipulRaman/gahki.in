FAQ

Quick answers.

Do I need any hardware?

No. A phone with a camera works. If you have a USB barcode scanner, even better — it acts as a keyboard and Gahki picks it up natively.

Will it work offline?

Gahki is an online platform — your stock stays in sync across the counter and online store. A short outage during billing is queued client-side and retried automatically.

Is my data safe?

Yes. Each shop's data is isolated, sign-in is by email code only (no passwords to leak), and we run on enterprise cloud infrastructure with daily backups.

What about GST & tax?

The price you set on each product is the final price your customer pays. The tax portion is shown on the invoice for their records — we never add tax on top at checkout, so there are no last-minute surprises.

How much does it cost?

Two plans: POS at ₹499/mo (counter billing, 200 products, 1,000 bills/month, 3 staff) and POS + Store at ₹1,499/mo (online storefront, 2,000 products, 10,000 bills/month, 10 staff). Limits reset on the 1st of every month (IST). Need different limits or multi-shop? Ask us about a custom plan.

Can my customers re-order?

Yes. Flip on the storefront from your shop settings, share the shop.gahki.in/your-shop link, and customers can browse and place COD orders. You confirm them from the POS app.
